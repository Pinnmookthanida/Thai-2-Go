Welcome to Thai 2 Go

At Thai 2 Go, we are dedicated to serving the best quality and most authentic Thai food. Our menu is carefully crafted to showcase the rich and diverse flavors of Thai cuisine, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.We believe that food is more than just sustenance. It is a reflection of culture, history, and identity. That's why we are passionate about serving authentic Thai cuisine that celebrates the rich and diverse flavors of our homeland.

We believe that food should be made with care and attention to detail, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. Our dishes are prepared with traditional Thai cooking techniques, passed down through generations of chefs, and infused with the love and passion that we bring to our craft.

So come and join us at Thai 2 Go, and let us take you on a culinary journey to the vibrant streets of Thailand.

Our Menu



For entrees, we have a wide selection of stir-fries and noodle dishes, each with their own unique combination of spices, herbs, and vegetables.
Some of our most popular dishes include our Pad Thai, a stir-fry with rice noodles, eggs, tofu, bean sprouts, and a tamarind sauce,
and our Pad Kra Pao, a stir-fry with minced pork, holy basil, and chilli.

We also offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options, such as our Vegetable Stir Fry, and our Vegan Kao Pad.

To top it off, we have a selection of refreshing beverages such as our Thai Tea.

Delivery


Everyday Meals


At Thai 2 Go, we understand that you may not always have the time to stop by our food truck in person. Due to this, we provide a convenient delivery service that sends our delicious original Thai food right to your door. You aren't even required to leave the comfort of your home or office to eat all of your favourite dishes—just a few clicks will do it.

In order to fulfil all of your delivery requirements, consider Thai 2 Go whether you're searching for a fast lunch, a family meal, or a great snack to share with friends. Regardless of your location, we're committed to giving you the greatest dining experience we can. Thai 2 Go delivered to your door is made possible with Deliveroo, Uber Eats and Just Eat.

Pre-Order and Pickup:


Pre-ordering from Thai 2 Go is a simple and convenient way to ensure that you receive your favorite dishes quickly and efficiently.
